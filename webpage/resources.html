What do the tags in our website mean?

Treats patient respectfully as autonomous individuals

The doctor will treat you as an independent autonomous individual irrespective of your caste, creed, religion, marital status, sexual preferences and choices.
They will look for solutions to your pain and illness without advocating marriage or pregnancy.
They will respectfully and non-judgmentally ask about and accept your sexual activity and history, regardless of marital status.
They will discuss choosing to stay celibate or child-free positively and non-judgementally.

Trans-friendly

The doctor will make trans men, trans women and other people who don't identify as cis female feel welcomed and discuss gender dysphoria related medical issues positively and non-judgmentally.

Queer-friendly

The doctor will discuss queer sexuality positively and non-judgementally.

Treats survivors of abuse sensitively and respectfully

The doctor will welcome survivors of incest, sexual and domestic abuse, and discuss healing from rape and sexual trauma sensitively and respectfully.

AIDS/Other STDs-friendly

TThe doctor will welcome people with AIDS or other sexually transmittable diseases without judgement.

Body Positive

The doctor will examine your body thoroughly and professionally without making you feel ugly, embarrassed, ashamed or uncomfortable.
